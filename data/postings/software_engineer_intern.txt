JOB TITLE: Software Engineer Intern (Fall 2026)

COMPANY: Innovatech Solutions

LOCATION: Helsinki, Finland (Hybrid)

ABOUT US:
Innovatech Solutions is a fast-growing B2B SaaS company specializing in AI-driven analytics. Our engineering team is the heart of our product, building scalable and robust solutions using Python, Go, and React. We believe in mentorship and giving interns real, impactful work.

JOB DESCRIPTION:
We are looking for an enthusiastic Software Engineer Intern to join our core product team. This is a 3-6 month paid internship. You will work alongside senior engineers, contributing to our codebase, participating in the full development lifecycle, and learning what it takes to build software at scale.

RESPONSIBLITIES:
* Write, test, and debug code for our web applications and data pipelines.
* Assist in fixing bugs and implementing new features.
* Participate in team meetings, code reviews, and Agile ceremonies (sprints, stand-ups).
* Learn our tech stack (Python, React, AWS) and development processes.
* Collaborate with a mentor to set learning goals and work on a defined intern project.

QUALIFICATIONS:
* Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related technical field.
* Must be graduating in 2026 or 2027.
* Solid understanding of fundamental computer science concepts (data structures, algorithms).
* Programming experience in at least one object-oriented language (e.g., Python, Java, C++) or JavaScript.
* Eagerness to learn, a strong work ethic, and good problem-solving skills.
* Good communication skills in English.

PREFERRED (Nice-to-have):
* A personal GitHub profile or portfolio with projects you can show.
* Familiarity with version control (Git).
* Basic knowledge of web development (HTML/CSS/JS) or cloud services (AWS, GCP, Azure).
