JOB TITLE: Customer Service Intern (Summer 2026)

COMPANY: ConnectSphere Inc.

LOCATION: Helsinki, Finland (Hybrid)

ABOUT US:
ConnectSphere is a leading provider of cloud-based communication tools for remote teams. We believe in making collaboration seamless and intuitive. Our customer support team is at the heart of our success, ensuring our users feel heard and valued.

JOB DESCRIPTION:
We are looking for an energetic and empathetic Customer Service Intern to join our support team. This is a 3-month paid internship. You will be the first point of contact for many of our users, learning to resolve issues, escalate problems, and ensure everyone has a great experience with our product. No prior experience is needed!

RESPONSIBLITIES:
* Respond to customer inquiries via email and live chat.
* Learn to use our support software (e.g., Zendesk) to document interactions.
* Troubleshoot basic user issues with a friendly and patient attitude.
* Gather customer feedback and share insights with the product team.
* Assist in writing and updating our public FAQ and help-center articles.

QUALIFICATIONS:
* Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree (any field of study is welcome).
* Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.
* Strong empathy and a genuine passion for helping others.
* A positive attitude and a strong desire to learn new skills.
* Comfortable with technology and learning new software applications.

PREFERRED (Nice-to-have):
* Any experience in a customer-facing role (e.g., retail, food service, volunteer work) is a plus, but not required.
* Interest in the tech or SaaS industry.
