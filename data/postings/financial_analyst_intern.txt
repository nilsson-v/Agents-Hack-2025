JOB TITLE: Financial Analyst Intern (Summer 2026)

COMPANY: Innovatech Solutions

LOCATION: Helsinki, Finland (Hybrid)

ABOUT US:
Innovatech Solutions is a fast-growing B2B SaaS company specializing in AI-driven analytics. Our finance team is crucial to our strategic growth, providing the data and insights that drive our business decisions.

JOB DESCRIPTION:
We are seeking a highly analytical and detail-oriented Financial Analyst Intern. This internship offers hands-on experience in financial planning & analysis (FP&A), budgeting, and reporting. You will support the team in analyzing financial data, tracking key metrics, and preparing reports for leadership.

RESPONSIBLITIES:
* Assist in the preparation of monthly and quarterly financial reports.
* Help with the annual budgeting and rolling forecast processes.
* Analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) and financial trends.
* Support ad-hoc data analysis requests from the finance and leadership teams.
* Learn to use our financial software (e.g., NetSuite, Tableau).

QUALIFICATIONS:
* Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Economics, Accounting, or a related quantitative field.
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
* Proficient in Microsoft Excel (e.g., VLOOKUP, pivot tables, basic modeling).
* Basic understanding of the three financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow).
* High attention to detail and strong organizational skills.

PREFERRED (Nice-to-have):
* Previous internship experience in finance or accounting.
* Experience with SQL or a data visualization tool (Tableau, Power BI).
* It is very important that the person has previous work experience
